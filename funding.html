Government funding

What Canada is putting behind AI adoption.

Under Canada's national AI for All strategy (June 2026), the federal government is backing business AI adoption with over $2 billion in new investment, including programs aimed squarely at small and medium businesses.

SME financing

LIFT, $500M via BDC

A $500 million SME financing program (LIFT), delivered through BDC, to help smaller businesses get off the AI sidelines.

Compute access

AI Compute Access Fund

A $700 million expansion (toward $1 billion total) for affordable, sovereign Canadian compute.

Skills

Free AI literacy training

National investment in free AI literacy and skills training for Canadians and their teams.

Sources: Government of Canada national AI strategy (ISED, June 4, 2026) and BDC. Confirm details on the official strategy page and BDC's LIFT announcement; eligibility and amounts can change.
