Case study: Alebex enrolment automation.

Challenge

After-hours leads going cold

High inquiry volume and slow follow-up meant prospective students slipped away.

Solution

Voice AI with human escalation

Alebex handled inbound and outbound voice, qualification, and booking, with escalation for sensitive cases.

Results

Measured, not promised

78% appointment rate with qualified prospects; 6× lower cost per acquisition; 60–74% contact and 42–50% booking rates at 5–10× lower cost than manual outreach.
