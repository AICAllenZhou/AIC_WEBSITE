Why a readiness review first

Canada's adoption gap isn't a technology gap. It's a clarity gap.

Organisations that skip assessment buy tools that don't fit, train people on the wrong things, and lose trust internally. The review gives you clarity and confidence, in writing, before any larger commitment, so every dollar you spend afterward is aimed at a real opportunity.

Quick answers

Common questions.

How much does the AI Readiness Review cost?

The initial consultation is free. The AI Readiness Diagnostic is a paid engagement scoped to your organisation's size; pricing is confirmed before any work begins.

How long does it take?

The initial consultation takes about an hour. The AI Readiness Diagnostic for a typical SME is completed within two to three weeks.

Do we have to buy anything afterward?

No. You leave with a report and a recommended next step that is yours to keep, even if that step isn't with AIC.
