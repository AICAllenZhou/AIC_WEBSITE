Quick answers

Common questions.

How can a small business in Canada start using AI?

Start with an assessment, not a tool. AIC's AI Readiness Review identifies your highest-value opportunities, the training your team needs, and possible funding pathways under Canada's AI for All strategy, before you spend on technology.

Is AI adoption support available outside Vancouver?

Yes. AIC delivers training and adoption support in person across BC's Lower Mainland and virtually for organisations anywhere in Canada.

Will AI replace my staff?

Our approach augments people, not replaces them. We automate repetitive, high-volume work so your team can focus on judgment, relationships, and growth, with human oversight built in.
